Fortnite: Genre: Battle Royale, Sandbox Description: Fortnite is a free-to-play multiplayer online game where players compete in a battle royale format, either alone or in squads. The game features building mechanics, allowing players to construct structures using in-game materials. Developer: Epic Games Release Date: 2017

Minecraft: Genre: Sandbox, Survival Description: Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to explore, gather resources, build structures, and interact with the environment. The game has various modes, including Survival, Creative, and Adventure. Developer: Mojang Studios Release Date: 2011

Among Us: Genre: Social Deduction Description: Among Us is a multiplayer game where players work together on a spaceship, completing tasks, while trying to identify and vote out the "impostors" among them who are trying to sabotage the mission. Developer: InnerSloth Release Date: 2018

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild: Genre: Action-Adventure Description: Breath of the Wild is an open-world action-adventure game set in the fantasy world of Hyrule. Players control Link as he embarks on a quest to defeat the evil Calamity Ganon. Developer: Nintendo Release Date: 2017

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V): Genre: Action-Adventure, Open World Description: GTA V is an open-world game set in the fictional city of Los Santos. Players can explore the city, engage in various activities, and follow the stories of three different protagonists. Developer: Rockstar North Release Date: 2013

Call of Duty: Warzone: Genre: Battle Royale, First-Person Shooter Description: Call of Duty: Warzone is a battle royale game mode within the Call of Duty franchise. Players drop into a map, gather resources, and engage in combat until only one team or player remains. Developer: Infinity Ward, Raven Software Release Date: 2020

Animal Crossing: New Horizons: Genre: Life Simulation Description: In Animal Crossing: New Horizons, players build and customize their own island paradise. They can fish, catch bugs, design their home, and interact with anthropomorphic animal villagers. Developer: Nintendo Release Date: 2020

League of Legends: Genre: Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) Description: League of Legends (LoL) is a team-based game where players control "champions" with unique abilities and work together to destroy the enemy team's Nexus. Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO): Genre: First-Person Shooter Description: CS:GO is a competitive shooter where players join either the terrorist team or the counter-terrorist team to complete objectives or eliminate the other team. Overwatch: Genre: First-Person Shooter, Team-Based Description: Overwatch is a team-based shooter with a cast of diverse heroes, each with unique abilities and roles. Players work together to complete objectives while countering the opposing team's strategies. Developer: Blizzard Entertainment Release Date: 2016

Pokémon GO: Genre: Augmented Reality, Mobile Description: Pokémon GO is an augmented reality game that encourages players to explore the real world to catch virtual Pokémon creatures. It uses location-based technology to blend gaming with real-world exploration. Developer: Niantic, The Pokémon Company Release Date: 2016

Red Dead Redemption 2: Genre: Action-Adventure, Open World Description: Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world game set in the late 1800s, focusing on the story of Arthur Morgan, an outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang. FIFA Series: Genre: Sports (Soccer) Description: The FIFA series is a collection of soccer (football) simulation games that allow players to control and manage teams in various modes, including career mode, Ultimate Team, and online multiplayer matches. Assassin's Creed Valhalla: Genre: Action-Adventure, Open World Description: Assassin's Creed Valhalla is set in the Viking Age and follows the story of Eivor, a Viking raider.
